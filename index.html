The early weeks with your newborn are a time of profound change — joyful, emotional, and often overwhelming. With more than 35 years of experience as a Midwife and 25 years as an International Board-Certified Lactation Consultant, I provide personalised postnatal and breastfeeding support that helps you feel confident, informed, and truly cared for.

My approach is grounded in listening deeply, respecting your goals, and offering clear, evidence-based guidance. Whether you're navigating feeding challenges, adjusting to life with your baby, or seeking reassurance in the postnatal period, I walk alongside you with warmth, professionalism, and continuity of care.

You deserve support that feels safe, respectful, and centred entirely around your needs. It is a privilege to be part of your journey.
